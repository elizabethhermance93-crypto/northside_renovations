reCAPTCHA setup for Request an Estimate form
=============================================

The form uses Google reCAPTCHA v2 ("I'm not a robot" checkbox) to reduce spam.

CURRENT: Test keys are in place so the form works immediately. For production,
replace them with your own keys so only real users can submit.

1. Get your keys (free):
   https://www.google.com/recaptcha/admin
   - Register your domain (e.g. northsiderenovations.com)
   - Choose "reCAPTCHA v2" > "I'm not a robot" Checkbox
   - You'll get a Site Key and a Secret Key

2. Update the Site Key (front-end):
   - File: request-an-estimate.html
   - Find: id="estimate-recaptcha" data-sitekey="..."
   - Replace the data-sitekey value with your Site Key

3. Update the Secret Key (back-end):
   - File: assets/inc/sendmail.php
   - Find: $recaptcha_secret = '...'
   - Replace with your Secret Key (keep it private)

After updating both keys, the form will block bots and allow real submissions.
